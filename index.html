How to Custom Tesla Lock Sound?

  1. Download an audio file you like, Rename it to LockChime.wav and Copy it into the root directory of your Tesla USB flash drive.

  2. Plug the USB stick back into your car’s glovebox, then head to Toybox > Boombox > Check Lock Sound Select USB from the drop down menu.

Now, when you lock your Tesla, your custom sound you’ve downloaded should play. Time to impress your friends and family!
